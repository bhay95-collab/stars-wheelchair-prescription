Use these as the guidance basis only. The tool still requires clinical judgement, trial confirmation, supplier specification checks and local funding review.

How to use this tool

What this is. A structured clinical-reasoning aid for wheelchair and seating prescription. It is decision support, not a record in itself, and does not replace clinical judgement, trial, senior review or supplier specifications.

When and by whom. Intended for the prescribing clinician across an admission. It is not a single-sitting form — complete sections as the assessment unfolds (measurements and MAT early, trial and supplier detail later). Sections start minimised to reduce overload; expand only the part you are working on.

Working across multiple clinicians. Use Save progress to export your entries as a file, and Load progress to reopen it (or hand it to a covering clinician). This supports continuity when the primary clinician is on leave or rotates out. Data stays on your device — nothing is uploaded.

Documenting in iEMR. Use Create to generate the text for your records — "Create documentation to paste into medical record" produces a clean, ready-to-paste summary of everything entered so far (only completed fields are included), and "Create vendor email template" produces the supplier-facing brief. This is the intended way to get information from this tool into iEMR. Printing remains available under Print options if a hard copy is needed for a specific purpose, but it is not the primary documentation pathway.

Auto-calculated dimensions are starting points only — always check against posture, pressure care, transfers, propulsion and supplier measurement conventions, and confirm at trial.

How this maps to the Equipment Workflow. The six Parts follow the phases of the STARS Equipment Workflow tool — each Part header shows its matching workflow phase, so the two tools can be used side by side.

Product reference. For cushions, seating and positioning options, the national equipment database (AskNED) is a useful reference: AskNED — wheelchair cushions, seating and positioning.
